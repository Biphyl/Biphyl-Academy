About

Biphyl Academy is a NGO kind of organization that deals with capturing young talents in the communities. The youths targeted are in between the age frame of 10-25years. This ensures that any young person that has talent that can be tapped and natured is given an opportunity to stand out and be able to polish on his/her talent. We have a ll the things that a talented youth can need in order to move forwarrd in terms of their talents.

We look into different fields of talents like on the field games like football, basketball and rugby. We also deal with those talented in music and arts. The environment in which the youths get a chance to showcase their talents and then then given an opportunity to develop and sharpen their skills is very cool and calm.

If you are interested tp join us or you have seen someone with the potential on a certain talent, fo not hesitate to contact us or direct the person to our offices to register with us. Our offices are located in Kasabuni along Babadogo Road in Orange House plaza 3rd floor door no. 15.

Games

Football

In the game of football, we acormodate both ladies and men of the ages descibed in the paragraph above. The organization provides the players with all the things they need in the field in order to make sure there is no blockage to sharpening of their skills. Whenever a player is injured in the pitch there are trained and qualified medical doctors that will definitely take care of the injury. The medical staff have all the things that is needed incase a player is injured. When a player is seen to be joking mostly during practices or during the games he or she will be suspended from the team for a period of time and when the player does the same kind of mistake more than three times we dont hesitate to expel the player completely.

Basketball

In the basketball side we have proffessional trainers that are ready to make the players achieve the best they can in the basketball court and also enjoys the game. There are all the requirements that a player can ask for and because of this we do not expect any player to miss on the trainings unless with a valid reason. In the course of the training periods there are some friendly matches that are to be played to make the players get used to the game either home or away

Rugby

This is a very tough game but there are highly trained to be maximum level and also the players are taught on how to maintain there body balance and also taught on the type of diet someone should have for body fitness.
